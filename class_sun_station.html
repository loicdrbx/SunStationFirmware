void SunStation::computeCarbonSaved ( )

Computes the amount of carbon (kg of CO2) saved by the SunStation in its lifetime. Energy to carbon emissions conversion factor from the EPA: 7.09 × 10^-4 metric tons CO2/kWh -> 7.09 × 10^-4 kg CO2/Wh https://www.epa.gov/energy/greenhouse-gases-equivalencies-calculator-calculations-and-references
